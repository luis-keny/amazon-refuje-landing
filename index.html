REFUGE

AMAZON

It is a unique and visionary initiative in the Peruvian Amazon region. This project has been conceived with the objective of offering a safe, sustainable, and prosperous environment for families seeking an escape from global tensions, while also representing an excellent investment opportunity.

ABOUT

US

It is located in one of the most biodiverse and protected regions on the planet, offering a unique opportunity to live in harmony with nature.

The project is designed to ensure the comprehensive safety of families by providing access to an inexhaustible source of water and natural resources, ensuring long-term self-sufficiency.

It offers opportunities to generate sustainable income through ecotourism and the rental of country houses, allowing owners not only to enjoy a privileged environment but also to take advantage of the growth of tourism in the region.

As part of our sustainability vision, Refugio Amazonas Madre de Dios will have 3 Germplasm Banks aimed at ensuring food security and promoting the preservation of native species, contributing to global biodiversity and the development of sustainable agricultural practices.

WE ARE

HERE

Located in the city of Puerto Maldonado, Tambopata Province and District, Madre de Dios Department, we have an area of 61 hectares and/or 150.734 acres. We have 406 meters of river frontage with the Madre de Dios River, the largest navigable river in the region.

OUR

DIFFERENTIAL

373

ECO HOUSES

Physical and Food Security for You and Your Family

"Refugio Amazónico Madre de Dios" features 1000 m2 plots with self-sufficient and sustainable country houses. These houses will provide energy and food independence.

We will have a tour operator that will generate residual income from the visits and stays of tourists in our Eco Village.

27 HECTARES OF GERMPLASM BANK

We have 03 germplasm banks, each 9 hectares, strategically located to safeguard food independence throughout the eco village. In the germplasm bank, we will have medicinal plants, fruit trees, and timber trees.

ECO VILLAGE

The property area is 1000 m2, with a proposed country house of 80 m2.

Kitchen, dining room, living room, 3 bedrooms, a full bathroom, laundry room, and terrace.

The property features fruit trees such as coconut, mango, papaya, and others as needed or desired.

We have medicinal plants like Cat's Claw (for cancer), san (for infections), Sacha Jergon (for cancer treatment or immune system strengthening), and a wide range of herbal remedies.
